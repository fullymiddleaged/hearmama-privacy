# Privacy Policy

**Effective:** 8 May 2026
**Last updated:** 8 May 2026

## In plain English

**Womb Tones runs entirely on your phone.**

We don't have a server. We don't have an account system. We never see your name,
your email, or your card details. The voice clips you record stay on your
device. The audio files you choose to download stay on your device.

What is shared with someone else:

- **The free version shows ads** served anonymously by Google AdMob.
- **If you buy the lifetime upgrade**, Apple or Google handles the payment.
  We use a service called RevenueCat to track whether you have premium —
  it sees an anonymous device identifier, the country your store account
  is in, and the fact that a purchase happened. Nothing else.

That's the whole story. The rest of this document is the formal version of
the same thing.

If you have any questions, email us at
[support@easyannounce.com](mailto:support@easyannounce.com).

## 2. Information we collect

### From you, directly

**None.** We don't ask for your name, email address, phone number, location,
or any other personally identifying information. There is no sign-up.

### Automatically

**None.** The app does not include analytics, telemetry, crash reporting,
fingerprinting, or any other automatic data collection from us.

---

## 3. Information stored on your device

The app stores the following on your device only. This information never
leaves your device unless explicitly noted:

- **Onboarding answers**: heart rate, gestational age, fibre level, BMI,
  baby position, device type. Used to compute the audio parameters for
  your personalized soundscape.
- **Voice clips** (mum's voice, dad's voice — premium): stored in memory
  only during a session if you upload or record one.
- **Premium status**: a single boolean flag indicating whether you've
  purchased the lifetime upgrade.
- **Wellness disclaimer acknowledgement**: a single boolean flag.
- **WAV files** that you choose to download (premium): saved to the
  Documents folder of your device by your request.

You can delete all of this by tapping "Start over" in the app or by
uninstalling the app.

---

## 4. Microphone

We request microphone permission only when you tap "Record" to capture a
voice clip. The recording is held in your phone's memory, mixed into
your personalised soundscape, and discarded when you reset the app or
close it. **It is never uploaded.**

You can deny microphone permission and the rest of the app continues to
work normally.

---

## 5. Premium purchases (lifetime upgrade)

The lifetime upgrade is sold via Apple's In-App Purchase or Google Play
Billing. Payment is handled entirely by Apple or Google according to their
own privacy policies — **we never see your card details or billing address**.

To track whether your device has the entitlement, we use
[RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) — a widely used purchase
infrastructure service. RevenueCat receives:

- An **anonymous device identifier** that is generated locally and is not
  linked to your real identity
- The **fact that a purchase happened** (yes/no)
- Your **platform** (iOS or Android)
- Your **country** (from your App Store / Play Store account)

RevenueCat does **not** receive your real name, email address, or precise
location.

If you reinstall the app on the same store account, the "Restore Purchase"
button asks Apple or Google to confirm your purchase, and RevenueCat
re-grants premium access.

## 6. Advertising (free version only)

The free version of Womb Tones shows occasional interstitial ads served by
**[Google AdMob](https://policies.google.com/privacy)**. AdMob may collect:

- Anonymous device advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID, IDFA)
- Approximate location at a country/region level
- Basic device metadata (type, OS version, screen size)
- Ad interaction events

On iOS 14.5+, AdMob will ask for your permission via the **App Tracking
Transparency** prompt before personalizing ads to you. If you choose
"Ask App Not To Track", you'll continue to see ads but they will be
non-personalized.

The premium version has no ads.

## 8. Children's privacy

Womb Tones is designed to be played **for** babies by adult parents and
caregivers. The app is **not directed at, or marketed to, children
under 13** as a user. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

If you are a parent or caregiver and believe a child has interacted with
the app in a way that resulted in data being collected, please contact us
and we will assist where possible (although we hold no personal data
ourselves).

## 9. Your rights

### New Zealand (Privacy Act 2020)

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have rights including:

- The right to access information held about you
- The right to correct that information
- The right to make a complaint to the
  [Office of the Privacy Commissioner](https://www.privacy.org.nz/)

Because we do not collect or store any personally identifying information
about you, there is nothing for us to provide in response to an access
request. To delete the information that exists on your own device,
uninstall the app or use the "Start over" reset within it.

### European Union and United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the EU or UK, the General Data Protection Regulation gives
you similar rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection,
and data portability. As above, we hold no personally identifying
information so there is no data for us to operate on.

### California (CCPA)

If you are in California, the California Consumer Privacy Act gives you
similar rights. We do not sell or share personal information.
